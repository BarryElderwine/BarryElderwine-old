Summer Showers Shimmer
Is Mother's Touch
To remind us
to look through the artifice
Clowered Castles
and Misdirection
the surface of our ambled ways
----- Chorus----
Dawn's Light
Paints a picture
No lines
Emerging Colors
Three sides
forgiving, grateful, giving
revealing
A beautiful wonder
