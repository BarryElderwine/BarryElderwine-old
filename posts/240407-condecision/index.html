Condecision

April 7, 2024
Categories: Personal, Humanity

The following is a running list of words inspired by The Dictionary of Obscure Sorrows  by John Koenig

Condecision is state where decision making is impeded for an awareness that whatever decision is made the consequences are likely to have a net negative outcome.

Emplode: An occurrence when ones empathic sensibilities contribute to a state condecision to the point of emotional, cognitive and behavioral collapse.

!### A special case of emplosion when ones empathic sensibilities are spread across 2 or more others who are in conflict each

!@@@ A special case of !### where you are involved in the circumstances of the conflict.
